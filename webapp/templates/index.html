DnD Spell Explorer

Welcome to our DnD Page!

This project was meant to be a tool for new and experience DnD players alike. We wanted it to be a quick and easy way to access information about a variety of aspects of the game. Through some trial and error we realized that we should focus our efforts on making spells the primary focus. With that, we have made tables of all the spells in the 5th Edition Player's Handbook as well as tables that split the spells by classes. These tables can be filtered and searched through. You also have the ability to click the name of a spell and see more information about it. This information is not the prettiest but is accurate.
