Personal Story

I should say that my real reason for filling this assignment up with dog and cat combo propaganda is because it's a life-long dream of mine.

Picture this: You go to a shelter, looking to take home an adorable pupper... You walk up and down the rows of cages, past the adorable stares and occascional choars of barks until you find the one. Storybook connection, maybe love at first sight is more appropriate?

You are getting all set to take this new family member home when suddenly, out of the corner of your eye you see a little kitty. Staring at you, and your new best friend, with big ole eyes (think Puss in Butts level). At that moment you realize, you need them in your life too. You take them home too, two brand new friends for you and for each other.
